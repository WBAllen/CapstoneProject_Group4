PRIDE - Personal Responsibility in a Desirable Environment

What is Kentucky Pride?

  • Our vision is to restore the natural beauty of Southern and Eastern Kentucky
  • We provide citizens with the tools, education, and resources to take responsibility for our shared environment.
  • We strive to enhance the economic and cultural growth of the region through improving water quality, cleaning up solid waste problems, and advancing environmental education.
